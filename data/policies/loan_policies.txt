LOAN POLICIES - AXE FINANCE

================================================================================
CREDIT SCORE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
Minimum credit score: 600
Recommended credit score: 650 or higher
Excellent credit: 750 or higher

Score ranges:
- Excellent (750-850): Best rates, fast approval
- Good (700-749): Standard rates, normal processing
- Fair (650-699): Higher rates, additional documents may be required
- Poor (600-649): Limited products, guarantor may be required
- Below 600: Application likely rejected


================================================================================
DEBT-TO-INCOME RATIO (DTI)
================================================================================
Maximum DTI: 45% (0.45)
Recommended DTI: Below 40% (0.40)
Ideal DTI: Below 35% (0.35)

A guarantor is required if your credit score is below 650 or DTI is above 35%.


================================================================================
LOAN AMOUNTS
================================================================================
Minimum loan: 5,000 TND
Maximum loan: 500,000 TND
Personal loans: Up to 100,000 TND
Business loans: Up to 500,000 TND


================================================================================
LOAN TERMS
================================================================================
Minimum term: 12 months
Maximum term: 84 months (7 years)
Typical terms: 24, 36, 48, 60 months
Real estate loans: Up to 120 months


================================================================================
INTEREST RATES
================================================================================
Base rate: 10%
Range: 8% to 16% per year

Risk-based pricing:
- Low risk: 8% to 10%
- Medium risk: 10% to 12%
- High risk: 12% to 14%
- Very high risk: 14% to 16%

Personal loans: 9% to 14%
Business loans: 8% to 13%


================================================================================
FEES
================================================================================
Application fee: 1% of loan amount (minimum 50 TND, maximum 500 TND)
Early repayment: Allowed, no penalty
Late payment penalty: 2% per month on overdue amount
Grace period: 5 days before late payment applies
Express processing: 2-3 business days (additional fee applies)


================================================================================
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
================================================================================

Personal Loan Eligibility:
- Age: 21 to 65 years at end of loan
- Residency: Tunisian resident
- Employment: Employed minimum 6 months at current employer
- Minimum monthly net income: 800 TND
- Maximum DTI: 45%
- Minimum credit score: 600
- No active bankruptcy

Business Loan Eligibility:
- Business in operation: minimum 2 years
- Minimum annual revenue: 100,000 TND
- Positive net income for at least 1 of last 3 years
- DSCR minimum: 1.2
- Maximum debt-to-equity ratio: 3.0
- Tax compliance required (no outstanding tax debts)


================================================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
================================================================================

Personal Loans:
- National ID card (both sides, valid)
- Proof of income: last 3 salary slips (bulletins de salaire)
- Employment certificate (attestation de travail)
- Bank statements: last 3 to 6 months
- Proof of address (utility bill, not older than 3 months)

For self-employed:
- Last 2 years of tax declarations
- Business registration (patente)
- Last 6 months of business bank statements

Business Loans:
- National ID card of owner(s)
- Business registration certificate (registre de commerce)
- Business license
- Income statement (compte de résultat) - last 3 years
- Balance sheet (bilan) - last 3 years
- Bank statements - last 6 months
- Business plan
- Tax returns - last 2 years
- Tax compliance certificate (attestation de non-redevance fiscale)

For investment loans, additionally:
- Detailed investment project description
- Financial projections for 3 to 5 years
- Quotes or invoices for planned purchases


================================================================================
COLLATERAL
================================================================================
Personal loans: Usually unsecured
Business loans: May require collateral depending on amount and risk
Maximum LTV (Loan-to-Value): 80%


================================================================================
PROCESSING TIME
================================================================================
Standard applications: 5 to 7 business days
Express processing: 2 to 3 business days (additional fee)
Personal loan decision: within 5 business days of complete file
Business loan decision: within 10 to 15 business days of complete file
Funds disbursement: 2 to 3 business days after approval


================================================================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: What is the minimum credit score?
A: The minimum is 600. We recommend 650 or higher for better rates.

Q: What is the maximum loan amount?
A: Personal loans up to 100,000 TND. Business loans up to 500,000 TND.

Q: What are the interest rates?
A: Rates range from 8% to 16% depending on your risk profile. Personal loans are 9-14%, business loans 8-13%.

Q: How long does approval take?
A: Standard processing is 5-7 business days. Express processing is 2-3 business days.

Q: Can I repay early?
A: Yes, early repayment is allowed with no penalty.

Q: What happens if I pay late?
A: There is a 5-day grace period. After that, a 2% monthly penalty applies on the overdue amount.

Q: Do I need a guarantor?
A: A guarantor is required if your credit score is below 650 or your DTI ratio is above 35%.

Q: Can self-employed people apply?
A: Yes. Provide tax declarations for the last 2 years and 6 months of business bank statements instead of salary slips.

Q: What documents do I need for a personal loan?
A: National ID, last 3 salary slips, employment certificate, bank statements (3-6 months), and proof of address.

Q: What documents do I need for a business loan?
A: National ID, business registration, income statement, balance sheet, bank statements (6 months), business plan, and tax returns (2 years).

Q: What is the minimum loan term?
A: 12 months minimum, 84 months maximum (7 years). Real estate loans can go up to 120 months.

Q: Is collateral required?
A: Personal loans are usually unsecured. Business loans may require collateral with a maximum LTV of 80%.
